About Us

The story behind Instagram Follower Tracker

Instagram Follower Tracker was built out of a simple frustration: Instagram gives you no way to see who has unfollowed you, and every third-party app that claims to do it asks for your password — putting your account at risk.

We built a better alternative. One that uses only the official data export that Instagram generates for you, processes it entirely in your browser, and never asks for a single credential.
