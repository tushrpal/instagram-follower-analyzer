Why Most Follower Trackers Fail for Private Accounts

Almost every Instagram follower tracker on the market relies on one of two techniques. The first is scraping public profile pages — visiting your profile URL like a regular visitor would and extracting the followers list from the HTML. This works for public accounts, but for private accounts the followers list simply doesn't load unless the visitor is already an approved follower. The tool gets nothing back.

The second technique is to log into Instagram using your username and password, then use the authenticated session to fetch your followers list through Instagram's internal API. This approach works for private accounts — but at a steep cost. It violates Instagram's Terms of Service, exposes your password to a third party, and frequently results in account suspensions. Even when it works, the tool needs your credentials forever to keep updating.

Our Approach: The Official Data Export

Instagram gives every user — public, private, business, or creator — the legal right to download a full copy of their account data. This export includes the complete followers and following lists, with no API rate limits, no scraping, and no password required. It is the same data Instagram's own servers see.

Our tracker reads this export and runs all the same analyses that scraping-based tools run, but with three big advantages for private accounts. The data is complete (no missed accounts due to rate limiting). The accuracy is perfect (it comes from Instagram itself). And there is zero risk to your account because no login ever happens.

What You Can Do on a Private Account

  • See who unfollowed you — by comparing two exports from different dates.
  • Find non-followers — accounts you follow that don't follow you back.
  • See your fans — accounts that follow you but you don't follow back.
  • List mutual followers — true two-way connections.
  • Track pending follow requests — see who hasn't accepted your follow yet.
  • Export to CSV — keep a record of any list outside the app.

How to Get Started

  1. Open Instagram on your phone or browser while logged into your private account.
  2. Go to Settings → Your activity → Download your information → Request a download.
  3. Choose Some of your information and select only Followers and following.
  4. Set format to JSON, date range to All time, and submit.
  5. Wait 10–30 minutes for Instagram's email, then download the ZIP file.
  6. Upload the ZIP to our tracker and view your full dashboard.

Privacy Stays Private

Your private account remains private throughout the entire process. Nothing about your data is shared with any third party, no Instagram session is created, and no one outside of you sees your followers list. You don't need to switch your account to public at any point.
